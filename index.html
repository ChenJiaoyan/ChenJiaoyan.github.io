Biography

Since 11/2022, I have been a permanent Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Department of Computer Science, University of Manchester. I'm also an Academic Visitor of Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford.

Before that, I was a full-time Senior Researcher at Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford, led by Prof. Ian Horrocks, worked very shortly as a PostDoctoral Researcher in Heidelberg University. I got my Ph.D in Computer Science and Technology in Zhejiang University in 2016, supervised by Prof. Huajun Chen, and my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology in Zhejiang University in 2011. I was a visiting student of Department of Informatics, Zurich University from 2014 to 2015.

Research Interests

My research interests focus on knowledge graph, mainly including

  • Knowledge Graph Construction: the embedding, alignment, completion, population, quality assurance of knowledge graphs (OWL ontologies).
  • Semantic Table Understanding with knowledge graphs and machine learning.
  • Language Model: exploring language models for constructing knowledge graphs, and supporting semantic embedding and reasoning.
  • Machine Learning: knowledge-aware zero-shot and transfer learning, inductive knowledge graph completion, knowledge-augmented explanation.
