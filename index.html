Biography

Jiaoyan is a Senior Lecturer (~Associate Professor) in Department of Computer Science, The University of Manchester. Before that, he was a Lecturer in Manchester from 2022, a Senior Researcher in Department of Computer Science, University of Oxford from 2017 (with Prof. Ian Horrocks), and a postdoctoral in Heidelberg University. Jiaoyan got his PhD and bachelor degrees both in Zhejiang University on Computer Science and Technology. He was a visiting PhD in Department of Informatics, Zurich University from 2014 to 2015.

Research Interests

  • Knowledge Representation: construction and curation of knowledge graphs and ontologies, semantic techniques.
  • Machine Learning: knowledge representation learning, semantic embedding, neural-symbolic integration.
  • Data and Knowledge Management for AI: evaluating and augmenting large language models (LLMs) with knowledge graphs, ontologies and structured data.
